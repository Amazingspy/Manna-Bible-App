# 📖 Manna - Our Daily Bread

**Manna** is a high-fidelity Bible study and visualization platform designed to bridge the gap between ancient scripture and modern instructional design. By leveraging an AI-driven pipeline, Manna transforms raw scriptural insights into structured teaching materials and immersive visual experiences.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏛️ Multi-Translation Engine
Switch seamlessly between four cornerstone Bible versions, optimized for different study needs:
*   **NIV:** Modern and balanced for daily reading.
*   **ESV:** Literal and study-focused for deep exegesis.
*   **KJV:** Traditional, poetic, and majestic.
*   **NLT:** Highly readable and accessible for all ages.

### 🎨 Immersive Reading UX
*   **Dynamic Highlighting:** Multi-color verse marking with cloud-sync via Zoho Catalyst.
*   **Accessibility First:** Native pinch-to-zoom and fluid text scaling for all demographics.
*   **Rapid-Select Navigation:** A custom grid-based picker designed to reach any verse in under three taps.

### 📺 The Visualization Hub
*   **Book-Wise Overviews:** High-definition video summaries for every book (Genesis through Revelation), localized in heart-touching Tamil.
*   **Gospel-Centered Insights:** Specialized content tracing the "Yeshua" (Jesus) connection through the Old Testament, from Moses to the Judges.
*   **Instructional Design:** Content is processed through a structured pipeline to ensure pedagogical clarity.

### **Tech Stack used**

---

## 📜 Principles & Vision
Manna is built on the principle that the Word of God should be accessible, visual, and instructional. We use AI as a tool for content engineering, but maintain a "manual export" strategy to guarantee that the final output is grounded in truth and high-quality design.
